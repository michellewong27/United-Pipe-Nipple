About

UPN is a family owned pipe nipple manufacturer established in 1934. We have over 80 years of experience cutting and threading pipe for distribution to the plumbing, pipe, valve and fitting trades. We are located in Long Island, NY, just minutes away from the five boroughs of New York City, New Jersey and Connecticut. We maintain a large inventory for fast service and delivery at very competitive pricing. We ship throughout the United States and Internationally.

We specialize in the manufacturing and custom fabrication of pipe nipples made from carbon steel, red brass, stainless steel and aluminum pipe.

We have the capacity to custom fabricate pipe specifically to meet the needs of our customers. Thread One-End, BSPT Threads, NPSM Threads, Left Hand Threads, Grooved Ends, Beveled Ends, Reamed/Chamfered Ends can all be produced.

Full inventories of Malleable Iron Fittings (Black and Galvanized), Bronze Fittings (Brass), Stainless Steel Fittings, Merchant Steel Fittings, PVC Pipe & Fittings, Copper Tube & Fittings, Valves and Pex Tubing & Fittings, are all stocked in our warehouse, ready for immediate distribution.

Large stock of Steel Pipe ( Black & Galvanized) 10 Foot Long Threaded On Both Ends and Plain End and Red Brass Pipe 12 Foot Long Plain End. We also supply Stainless Steel (304 & 316) Pipe and Aluminum (T-6061)Pipe.

As a manufacturer since 1934, United Pipe Nipple has offered great service and quality Pipe, Pipe Nipples, Valves and Pipe Fittings at competitive prices.
